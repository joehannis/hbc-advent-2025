Mabbu

ABV: 6%

Brewer: Jimmy Dimitrov

Style: Hazy IPA

A new grist on my quest for Juicy Hazy IPA. On the plus side, lower EBC and more stable haze but the ABV came out short. Room for improvement next year.

A touch of bitterness from CTZ and dry-hopped with Amarillo, Citra, Mosaic and Simcoe.

Extra Pale, Malted Oats, Chit Malt, SafAle US05 & Lallemand (LalBrew) New England
