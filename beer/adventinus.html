Adventinus

ABV: 7%

Brewer: Joe Hannis

Style: Weizenbock/Doppelbock

My take on the classic Schneider Weisse Aventinus, the oldest Weizenbock still produced.

A rich, dark, strong wheat beer with a complex malt bill adding notes of dried fruit and liquorice, along with banana and clove from the yeast.

This was my first double decoction mash, which was fermented with Weihestephaner yeast. Serve at 7-10 degrees

Swirl the bottle before pouring!

Please return the bottle to the brewer.
