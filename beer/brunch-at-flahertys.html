Brunch At Flahertys 3.0

ABV: 9%

Brewer: Joe Hannis

Style: Imperial Stout

Brew club members who participated last year will remember my 'Breakfast at Flaherty's' Irish coffee stout. That stout has now been aged for 2 years, and as it has aged, the whiskey flavour has taken over. My new imperial stout takes this 2 year old, whiskey barrel aged coffee impy, and blends it with a new batch of young, sweeter imperial stout.

Please return the bottle to the brewer.
