Brunch At Flahertys

ABV: 9%

Brewer: Joe Hannis

Style: Imperial Stout

Brew club members who participated last year will remember my 'Breakfast at Flaherty's' Irish coffee stout. That stout has now been aged for 15 months, and as it has aged, the whiskey flavour has taken over. My new imperial stout takes this 15 month old, whiskey barrel aged impy, and blends it with a new batch of Japanese coffee imperial stout.

This new imperial stout is around 3 months old, and was dry beaned on Ueshima coffee from Japan for 36 hours.

Please return the bottle to the brewer.
