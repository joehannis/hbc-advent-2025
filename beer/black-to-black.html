Black To Black

ABV: 5.8%

Helen Gaynor

Black IPA

I’ve been meaning to make a Black IPA for years. Even bought ingredients a couple of times, but then done something else instead. So finally here we are.

This is an American IPA with Columbus & Centennial hops in the boil, dry hopped with Centennial & Chinook. Probably the most hops I’ve ever used in a brew.
