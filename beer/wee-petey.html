Wee Petey

ABV: 7.6%

Brewer: Stuart Lawton

Style: Wee Heavy

Tasting like whiskey in beer form, with a flavour profile of honey, caramel and peat smoke reminiscent of a single malt.

The malty backbone is complemented by fresh rosemary. This beer is best served at 10-12c in a thistle glass. Pairs well with lamb and game.
