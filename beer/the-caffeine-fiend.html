The Caffeine Fiend

ABV: 7%

Brewer: Ian Siviter

Style: Stout

For the 2024 HBC advent calendar I have brewed an export stout based on the beer brewed by legendary brewery The Kernel. The base beer was split in half, each of which were spiked with a tincture.

This version mixes the stout with a coffee and rum tincture. More information about the inspiration behind the original beer is given here
