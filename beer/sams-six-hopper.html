Sam's Six Hopper

ABV:4.7%

Brewer: The Jolly Maltster (Helen Gaynor) X SBC Beers

Style: IPA

Both my Advent Calendar beers were brewed with friends.

This 6-hop IPA was brewed with my American friend Samantha, with 6 different American hops - Amarillo, Chinook, Simcoe, Citra, Nugget & Galaxy, with 5 mixed hop additions in the boil & then dry-hopped. Malts include Vienna & caramel, and yeast was US05
