Death By Bock

ABV: 8%

Brewer: Dan Keeley

Style: Doppelbock

A rather sweet doppelbok that needs a lot longer to age. Nevertheless hits the 8% mark so go easy!

Utilises Munich malt from French and Jupps, as well as various other grains. Hops are irrelevant in this beer. It does capture the extreme maltiness of this style of beer quite well.
