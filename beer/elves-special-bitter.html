ESB

This beer is an alternate version to an ESB that I brew a lot. This time, the base recipe is Timothy Taylors Landlords, but with the grain bill bulked up while keeping the same percentages. F&J caragold has also been added, and this was mashed at a higher temperature to give a fuller body and sweeter finish.

The hops are Styrian Goldings and EKG, with an extra batch added at flamout. The yeast is a mix of WHC English Old Ale and Nottingham.

Please return the bottle to the brewer.
