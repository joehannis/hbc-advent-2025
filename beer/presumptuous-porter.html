Presumptuous Porter

ABV: 8.3%

Peter Robinson

London Porter

This beer is from a 1744 Durden Park recipe. With a predominance of brown and chocolate malts on the bouquet and in the flavour, the flavour should be full, with some residual sweetness to balance the hop and roast grains. The yeast went a bit mad so this beer is drier and more alcoholic than it should be.
