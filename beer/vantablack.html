Vantablack

ABV: 8.6%

Brewer: Stuart Lawton

Style: Imperial Stout

Brewed in July 2024 and continuing to mellow this full bodied imperial stout is perfect for a cold winters evening.

Aged on Plantation Rum barrel chips, this dark, rich beer adds notes of chocolate and rum along with dried fruits with a rich sweetness to balance it. This beer will continue to improve with age.

Best served in a snifter at 15c.
