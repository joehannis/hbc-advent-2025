Steve's Plum Porter

ABV:5%

Brewer: The Jolly Maltster (Helen Gaynor) X Barrington Brew Company

Style: Porter

Both my Advent Calendar beers were brewed with friends.

This plum porter was brewed with my friend Steve, and is supposed to be a clone of the famous Titanic one (not that I've ever tried that). The beer includes plums from his allotment, and Admiral & Bramling Cross hops. Base was Maris Otter with various specialty malts to add flavour & colour, and yeast was S04.
