Rye Doppelbock

ABV: 6.5%

Brewer: Gavin Dixon

Style: Doppelbock

Mostly Bohemian floor malted pilsner malt, 12% Munich II, 20% rye. So all the colour is from the rye. Yeast from the Oktoberfestbier.

Aroma hops Hallertau Tradition, dried overnight and added the day after harvesting.

Six weeks lagering for a rich malt profile.
