Melomel

ABV: 8%

Brewer: Jimmy Dimitrov

Style: Fruit Mead

Honey, dried fruit and fruit syrups. Bottle conditioned sparkling mead with some fruity goodness lingering near the bottom.

Shake gently a few minutes before opening and then open with care! A fruit cocktail of dates, coconut, appricots, cranberries and raisins with a dry finish.

Mead ABV can be as high as 18%, this one is really session strength!
